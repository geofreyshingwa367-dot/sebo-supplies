Our Journey & Foundation

Founded in 2020 by the visionary digital entrepreneur and systems engineering specialist, GEOFREY SHINGWA PETER, SEBO SUPPLIES & STATIONERY was established with a profound mission to bridge the critical gap between elite-level corporate administration materials and cutting-edge software solutions. The corporate title SEBO directly stands for SHINGWA EMPOWERMENT BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES—standing as a true testament to our core philosophy of creating corporate value, fostering socio-economic growth, and directly empowering individuals and educational enterprises through top-tier professional service delivery.

What originally launched as a dedicated localized venture widely known as GJ SUPPLIES & STATIONERY has systematically evolved through meticulous, high-grade rebranding into a massive tech-and-stationery powerhouse. Under the rigorous technical supervision of our founder, SEBO operates twin pillars of absolute excellence: **Next-Generation Stationery Logistics** and **Advanced High-Tier Software Engineering Support**. We do not merely offer basic retail options; we serve as your ultimate technological ally committed to delivering flawless speed, security, and exceptional corporate execution.

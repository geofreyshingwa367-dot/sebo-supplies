Founded in 2020 by the visionary digital entrepreneur and systems engineering specialist, GEOFREY SHINGWA PETER, SEBO SUPPLIES & STATIONERY was established with a profound mission to bridge the critical gap between elite-level corporate administration materials and cutting-edge software solutions. The corporate title SEBO directly stands for SHINGWA EMPOWERMENT BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES.

What originally launched as a dedicated localized venture widely known as GJ SUPPLIES & STATIONERY has systematically evolved through rebranding into a massive tech-and-stationery powerhouse. Under the technical supervision of our founder, SEBO operates twin pillars of absolute excellence: **Next-Generation Stationery Logistics** and **Advanced High-Tier Software Engineering Support**.
